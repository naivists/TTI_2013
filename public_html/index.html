About us

«GTravel» is a small, virtual travel organizer. We can help you keep travel documents such as airline tickets, hotel reservations and itineraries organized.

How does it work?

Once you register, you are assigned an automatic e-mail address yourname@gtravel.com. Just send the travel documents to this address and we'll do the rest.

Steps you need to take

  1. You buy the ticket online and wait for the confirmation e-mail
  2. Once you receive the e-mail, you forward it to yourname@gtravel.com
  3. We process the e-mail text an automatically extract the important data
    1. Date parser analyzes the numbers in text
    2. Location parser tries to identify geographical locations
    3. Hotel names are recognized, using Booking.com web service
  4. Voila!

Is it safe?

The system is fully automated, no humans are going to read your documents.

Personal data
We encrypt all the personal data received using contemporary cryptographic methods (AES-265, RSA)
If you are afraid about the personal data, you may delete sensitive information from the email body before you forward it to us.
Physical safety
Our servers are located in a bunker 90 feet under ground. Armed guards keep watch 24/7 and even longer, if necessary.
Service level agreement
We promise 99.99% uptime. All day, every day, except the four hours a year when nothing works.
